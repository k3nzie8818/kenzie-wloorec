Type of work: Social Media, Photography, Editing, Content Creation

Date: May 2024 - Present

Location: Waterloo, ON

Challenge

The @wloorec Instagram account maintained a consistent posting schedule and had plenty to promote, yet struggled with engagement. The posts relied heavily on repetitive templates, styles, and graphics, resulting in a lack of visual diversity and limiting the account’s growth potential.

Results

I introduced weekly posts aligned with current social media trends, shifting the focus from strictly informative content to engaging, relatable videos. The University of Waterloo is already a well-known and respected brand, so by expanding beyond their professional feel, we are able to connect with more of their target audience. This approach broadened the account’s reach, creating content that’s more interactive, shareable, and appealing to a wider audience. One of the reels reached 2.2 million likes, increased our interaction by 1289%, and has been shared 137k times, demonstrating the effectiveness of this strategy.
